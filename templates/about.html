Rakib Hasan

Photographer & Designer

Hello, I am Rakib Hasan, a fashion & portrait Photographer based in Bangladesh. I discovered my passion for art during childhood and became influenced by paintings and work of great photographers. I picked up a camera at the age of twenty and started capturing nature and landscape. I found a great interest in capturing human emotions and started taking portraits. Few years later I felt transition to doing it professionally. My body of work extends across portraits, Beauty, Fashion & capturing moments.
